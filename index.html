Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my e-Bay password?

You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot password" on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

Where is my order?

Log in to your e-Bay account, go to "My e-Bay" > "Purchase History" to track your orders and estimated delivery.

How do I return an item?

Visit the order in your Purchase History and select “Return this item.” Follow the instructions to print a return label.

Can I speak to a real person?

Yes! Just call our toll-free number 1-888-341-7651 and our team will be happy to help.
